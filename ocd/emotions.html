Good move, right? Well what emotions would OCD connect to? These are my
personal picks according the Inside Out Fandom's definitions.

1. Fear
Most of the obsessions and compulsions a person could have are based
on fears for something, whether its:
* Fear of germs
* Fear of losing something
* Obsessively washing hands

2. Anxiety
She gives feeling of tension, restlessness, worriness, and/or guilt
These are the feelings that people have that trigger their compulsions or obsessions

3. A little disgust
For fear of germs, you can see that they can be triggered by their disgust
